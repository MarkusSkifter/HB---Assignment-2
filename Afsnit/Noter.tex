\section{Model Setup and Parameters}

The model is a finite-horizon, deterministic life-cycle model of a dual-earner household. In each period $t = 0, \ldots, T-1$, both members choose hours of work. Wages depend on accumulated human capital, and the household is taxed on either joint or individual income depending on the tax regime. The full parameter values used throughout the assignment are listed in Table~\ref{tab:params}.
